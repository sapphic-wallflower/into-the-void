Black Bean and Corn Quesadillas

Notes:

You can use any beans you'd like. We've made this with black beans, and pinto beans so far.

Ingredients:













Instructions:

  1. In a medium skillet or wok, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil on medium heat.
  2. Once heated, combine the corn, beans, bell peppers, red onion, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for five minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Heat a large pan medium heat. Place one tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle 1/2 cup of shredded cheese on half of the tortilla.
  4. Spoon 1/2 cup of the mixture over the cheese on the tortilla. Fold the other half of the tortilla over to cover the filling.
  5. Press down gently on the quesadilla with a spatula and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until the tortilla is crispy and the cheese is melted. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling.
  6. Cut into 1/4th slices, and serve with your favourite toppings.
