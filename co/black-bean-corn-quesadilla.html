Black Bean and Corn Quesadillas

Notes:

You can most likely use any beans you'd like. We've made this with black beans, and pinto beans so far.

Ingredients:













Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the drained corn, black beans, diced bell peppers, red onion, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir well to mix all the ingredients together.
  2. Heat a large pan medium heat. Place one tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle about 1/3 cup of shredded cheese on half of the tortilla.
  3. Spoon about 1/2 cup of the corn and bean mixture over the cheese on the tortilla. Fold the other half of the tortilla over to cover the filling.
  4. Press down gently on the quesadilla with a spatula and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, until the tortilla is crispy and the cheese is melted. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling.
  5. Cut into 1/4th slices, and serve with your favourite toppings.
