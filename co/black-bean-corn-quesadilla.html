Black Bean and Corn Quesadillas

Ingredients:









Instructions:

  1. Drain and rinse a can of black beans and a can of corn.
  2. In a pan, cook the beans and corn with a little bit of oil and taco seasoning.
  3. Spread the mixture over a tortilla, then sprinkle with shredded cheese.
  4. Place another tortilla on top and cook on a pan until the cheese is melted and the tortillas are crispy.
  5. Serve with salsa and sour cream.
