Instructions

  1. Roast the Sweet Potatoes: Preheat your oven to 400°F (205°C). Wash and scrub the sweet potatoes, then prick them several times with a fork. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until they are fork-tender.
  2. Prepare the Black Bean Filling: While the sweet potatoes are baking, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the diced bell pepper and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add black beans to the skillet along with ground cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper. Cook for another 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally. Before turning off the heat, squeeze in the juice from the lime and stir well.
  4. Assemble the Stuffed Sweet Potatoes: Once the sweet potatoes are done baking, let them cool for a few minutes. Carefully slice each one open lengthwise, creating a pocket, but not cutting all the way through. Fluff the insides of the sweet potatoes with a fork. Then, stuff each potato with the black bean mixture.
  5. Garnish and Serve: Top with fresh cilantro and avocado slices, if using. Finish with a dollop of sour cream, if desired.
