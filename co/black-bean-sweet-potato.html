Instructions

Begin Baking

Pre-heat the oven to 350 F. Then, spray your baking sheet with cooking spray. After, place four sweet potatoes on the tray. Finally, put it in the oven.

Bake for 60 minutes.

Create Sauce


Insert above ingredients and whisk until saucey.

Prepare Toppings (Part 1)

50 minutes into the bake, add oil to your skillet. Then, once hot, add and saute onions for 5 minutes. After, add above spices and cook for another three minutes. Finally, add your black beans. Stir for five minutes, and remove from heat.

Add Toppings & Eat

Remove the sweet potatoes from the oven, and allow them to cool. Then, cut them open slightly, and mash the insides. Finally, add your mixture, avacado, cilantro, and your cream sauce.
