Black Bean Stuffed Sweet Potato

Ingredients:
















Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Wash and dry the sweet potatoes, then pierce each potato several times with a fork. Place the potatoes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for 45-60 minutes or until they are fork-tender.
  3. Meanwhile, in a large skillet over medium heat, sauté the onion, red bell pepper, and jalapeño pepper until the vegetables are soft and the onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the garlic, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Cook for 1-2 minutes or until fragrant.
  5. Add the black beans and 1/4 cup of water to the skillet. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 5-7 minutes or until the liquid has reduced and the beans are heated through.
  6. Remove the sweet potatoes from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes. Cut each potato in half lengthwise and scoop out a little of the flesh to make room for the filling.
  7. Fill each sweet potato half with the black bean mixture. Top with shredded cheddar cheese and return the potatoes to the oven. Bake for 5-7 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove the potatoes from the oven and top with diced avocado and chopped cilantro. Serve with lime wedges on the side.
