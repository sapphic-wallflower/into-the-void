Instructions

For the soup:

  1. Preperation: Cut onions into strips, and dice celery, red bell peppers, and garlic. Set aside.
  2. Cook Meat: Heat olive oil in a pan on medium-high heat and sear steak and add salt, then move to separate bowl with a slotted spoon to drain juices into pot.
  3. Cook Vegetables: In the remaining juices from the steak, sautée onions until slightly brown, or near caramelization. Then add bell peppers, celery, garlic, and white mushrooms and sautée until soft. Add black pepper as desired (fresh ground will really improve the flavor and fragrance).
  4. Create Soup Base: Add beef stock and bring to a boil, then move heat to medium-low and add in Velveeta and provolone.
  5. Finish Soup Base: Mix until completely dissolved into broth, then turn off heat and add half & half. Add salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Serve: Cut your steak into strips or cubes, and serve. Garnish with croutons and diced cherry tomatoes.

For the croutons:

  1. Cut the French bread into cubes.
  2. Brush with olive oil and add salt and pepper as desired.
  3. Broil bread on a tray until crispy.
