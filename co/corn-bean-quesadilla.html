Ingredients

4 large flour tortillas
1 can beans, drained
1 can of corn, drained
1 red bell pepper, diced
2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp chili powder
Salt and pepper to taste
2 tbsp olive oil or butter for cooking
Salsa, guacamole, and sour cream for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare Filling: In a large mixing bowl, combine the black beans, corn, diced bell pepper, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to ensure all the flavors meld together.
  2. Assemble Quesadillas: Lay out a tortilla on a flat surface. Sprinkle a layer of cheese on one half of the tortilla. Spread a generous amount of the bean and corn mixture over the cheese. Sprinkle another layer of cheese on top of the mixture. Fold the tortilla over to create a half-moon shape. Repeat with the remaining tortillas.
  3. Cook Quesadillas: Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add a bit of olive oil or butter. Place a filled tortilla in the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the tortilla is golden brown and the cheese has melted. Add more oil or butter as needed. Repeat with the remaining filled tortillas.
  4. Serve: Remove from the skillet and let cool for a minute. Using a sharp knife or pizza cutter, slice each quesadilla into wedges. Serve warm with salsa, guacamole, and sour cream on the side.
