Instructions

  1. Prep the Rice: If using freshly cooked rice, spread it out on a tray and let it cool completely to room temperature. Ideally, use rice that's been refrigerated overnight, as this will be less sticky and will fry better.
  2. Cook the Eggs: In a wok or large frying pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Pour in the beaten eggs and scramble them. Once cooked, transfer to a plate and set aside.
  3. Stir-Fry the Veggies: In the same wok or frying pan, add another tablespoon of oil and increase the heat to medium-high. Add the minced garlic, peas, and carrots. Stir-fry for a couple of minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the Rice: Increase heat to high, then add the rice to the wok or frying pan. Stir-fry the rice, making sure to break up any clumps, and mix well with the veggies.
  5. Season: Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir well to ensure the rice and veggies are evenly coated.
  6. Add the Eggs: Return the scrambled eggs to the wok or frying pan. Mix them into the rice.
  7. Finish and Serve: Add in the sliced green onions and give everything a final stir. Transfer to a plate or bowl, garnish with additional green onions or sesame seeds if desired, and serve immediately.
