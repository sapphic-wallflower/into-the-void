Instructions

  1. Cook Pasta: Cook pasta al dente, according to package directions.
  2. Blanch Broccoli: Blanch broccoli for 2 minutes in plenty of boiling water. Then soak broccoli into ice-cold water. Keep aside in a colander. You can omit this step if you like your broccoli super crunchy.
  3. Prepare Stir-Fry Sauce: In a mason jar, combine all ingredients for the stir-fry sauce. Screw on the lid tightly and shake vigorously to combine.
  4. Sauté Garlic and Broccoli: In a large skillet, heat two tablespoons of sesame oil to medium. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute, stirring often, until fragrant. Add broccoli florets and sauté for 2 – 3 minutes.
  5. Add Sauce: Add 1/2 cup of the stir fry sauce to the skillet and cook an additional 4 minutes, stirring often, until broccoli is cooked but still crunchy. Add a little water if the sauce gets too thick.
  6. Combine with Pasta: Add cooked, drained pasta to the skillet with the broccoli. Mix well to combine and reheat a little. Serve immediately, adding a little more sauce on top if desired.
