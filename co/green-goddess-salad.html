Ingredients

1 cucumber, diced
1/2 cabbage, diced
1 bunch of parsley
2 garlic gloves
3 tbsp of olive oil
1 tbps of maple syrup
1 lemon, juiced
A handful of cashews
Salt and chili flakes to taste
A little water

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dressing: In a blender or food processor, combine the parsley, garlic cloves, olive oil, maple syrup, cashews and lemon juice. Blend until you have a smooth mixture. Season with salt and chili flakes to taste. If the dressing is too thick, add a little water to reach your desired consistency.
  2. Assemble the Salad: In a large salad bowl, combine the diced cucumber and diced cabbage. Drizzle with the Green Goddess dressing, then toss gently to coat the ingredients in the dressing.
  3. Serve: Serve immdeiately with pita chips!
