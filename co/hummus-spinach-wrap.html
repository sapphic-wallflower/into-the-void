Instructions

  1. Prep the Ingredients: Ensure all vegetables are washed, dried, and thinly sliced. If you're using homemade hummus, have it ready.
  2. Assemble the Wrap: Lay out the tortilla wrap on a clean surface or a large plate. Spread an even layer of hummus over the entire surface of the wrap, leaving about a 1-inch border around the edges.
  3. Layer the Fillings: Start by placing a generous layer of fresh spinach leaves in the middle of the wrap. Add the sliced tomato, cucumber, and bell pepper.
  4. Season: Lightly season the veggies with salt and black pepper.
  5. Wrap it Up: Carefully fold in the sides of the tortilla and then roll it up from the bottom, ensuring all fillings are securely wrapped inside. Press gently to seal.
  6. Serve: Cut the wrap in half diagonally and enjoy immediately. If you're on the go, wrapping it in parchment paper can keep everything neatly together.
