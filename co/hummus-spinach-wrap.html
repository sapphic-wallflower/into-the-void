Hummus & Spinach Wrap

Ingredients:









Instructions:

  1. Lay the tortilla wrap flat on a plate.
  2. Spread a generous amount of hummus evenly over the entire tortilla.
  3. Layer fresh spinach leaves on top of the hummus, covering most of the tortilla's surface.
  4. Slice the avacado, cucumber, tomato, red onion, and red bell pepper into sticks.
  5. Add your veggies, then season with salt and pepper.
  6. Fold in the tortilla quarter to half way to the centre of the wrap.
  7. Roll the tortilla, holding in the sides while you do. Make sure your filling doesn't come out the other end of the roll.
  8. Serve. Additionally, you can slice in half and wrap in plastic wrap to serve later.
