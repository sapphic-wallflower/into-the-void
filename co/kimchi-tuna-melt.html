Instructions

  1. Prepare Tuna Mixture: In a bowl, mix the drained tuna, chopped kimchi, mayonnaise, and sesame oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Assemble Sandwiches: Spread a thin layer of mayonnaise on one side of each bread slice. On the non-mayo side of two slices, evenly distribute the tuna-kimchi mixture. Top with shredded cheese.
  3. Add Top Bread Slice: Place the remaining bread slices on top, mayo side out. This helps create a crispy, golden exterior when grilled.
  4. Grill the Sandwiches: Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Butter the outer sides of the sandwiches or add butter to the skillet. Grill the sandwiches for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  5. Serve: Cut the sandwiches in half and serve while hot.
