Instructions

Prepare Egg Whites

Whisk egg whites for a short while until they foam. Then, add 1/3 cup of sugar, and continue to beat until it's firm.

Prepare Yolk Mixture

Using a large bowl, add the above items. Then, whisk until fluffy and light-yellow. After, add half of the egg whites to the mixture and fold.

Prepare Yolk Mixture

Above the yolk mixture, sift in the above flour and powder in three portions, folding after each portion. Then, add the vegetable oil. Finally, add the remaining egg whites, folding gently.

Prepare & Bake

Line two of your pans with parchment paper alone the side and bottom. Then, pour the mix into your pans, and eliminate any large air bubbles. Finally, preheat to 350 F, bake for 27 to 30 minutes.
