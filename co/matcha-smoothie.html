Ingredients

1 cup unsweetened oat milk (or any milk of your choice)
1 tsp matcha powder
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 frozen banana, chopped
1 cup fresh spinach (packed)
A handful of ice cubes
1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional, for added sweetness)
Chia seeds or flaxseeds (optional, for added nutrition and texture)

Instructions

  1. Preparation: Make sure your banana is sliced and frozen. This will give the smoothie a creamy texture.
  2. Blending: In a high-powered blender, combine the oat milk, matcha powder, vanilla extract, frozen banana, and fresh spinach. If you like your smoothie a bit sweeter, you can add honey or maple syrup.
  3. Add Ice: Toss in a handful of ice cubes and blend until the smoothie is creamy and smooth. If you're using chia seeds or flaxseeds, you can add them now and give it a quick blend to mix them in.
  4. Serve: Pour your Matcha Vanilla Spinach Smoothie into a glass, and enjoy immediately! You can garnish with a sprinkle of matcha powder on top if you like.
