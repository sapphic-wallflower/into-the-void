Rainbow Veggie Sushi

Ingredients:











Instructions:

  1. Rinse the sushi rice, and place in medium saucepan with 1 1/3 cups of water.
  2. Place lid on saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then simmer. Cook rice for 15 minutes.
  3. Combine rice vinegar, sugar, and salt into a saucepan. Stir until sugar and salt disolve.
  4. Move rice to large bowl. Fold rice vinegar mixture into rice with wooden spoon.
  5. Lay a sheet of nori surface, shiny side down. Spoon 1/4 cup of rice onto the nori, using the back of the spoon to spread it evenly over the surface. Leaving a 1-inch border at the top.
  6. Arrange mix of veggies in a line across the center of the rice. Use fingers to press down on the veggies and rice to make them compact.
  7. To roll sushi, lift up the edge of the nori closest to you and use your thumbs to hold the fillings in place while rolling the nori away from you. Roll the sushi tightly, using the 1-inch border at the top to seal the roll closed.
  8. Use a sharp knife to slice each sushi roll into bite-sized pieces. Serve the sushi with soy sauce and wasabi on the side.
