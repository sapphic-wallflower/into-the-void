Instructions

  1. Prep the Sub Roll: Open the sub roll or baguette and lightly toast it in an oven or on a grill until it's slightly crispy on the inside.This step helps prevent the bread from becoming soggy.
  2. Layer the Sub:Begin by spreading a thin layer of Dijon mustard on both halves of the toasted roll.Start with the tomato slices on the bottom half, followed by the salami slices.Place the cheese slices on top.
  3. Season and Serve:Sprinkle a pinch of salt and black pepper over the assembled ingredients.Close the sub with the top half of the roll, press down gently to meld the ingredients together, and serve immediately.
