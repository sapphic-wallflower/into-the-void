Salami Sub

Ingredients:






Instructions:

Cold

  1. Slice the sub roll lengthwise, but not all the way through, creating a pocket for the fillings.
  2. Spread a layer of Dijon mustard on the inside of the bread.
  3. Layer the salami, cheese, tomato, red onion inside the sub.
  4. Close the sub, and enjoy.

Hot

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Slice the sub roll lengthwise, but not all the way through, creating a pocket for the fillings.
  3. Spread a layer of Dijon mustard on the inside of the bread.
  4. Layer the salami, cheese, tomato, red onion inside the sub.
  5. Close the sub roll and wrap it tightly in aluminum foil.
  6. Bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the bread is warmed through.
  7. Remove the sub from the oven and unwrap it from the foil. Enjoy
