Instructions

  1. Sausage Cooking: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sausage, and brown the sausage thoroughly and then remove it from the pot, setting it aside for later.
  2. Sauté Vegetables: In the same pot, add the diced onion, sliced carrots, and chopped celery. Sauté until the onions become translucent. Add the minced garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  3. Adding Main Ingredients: Return the browned sausage to the pot. Add the chopped cabbage, and diced tomatoes. Stir to combine.
  4. Broth and Seasonings: Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth. Add the bay leaf, dried thyme, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together, ensuring that the ingredients are well combined.
  5. Simmering: Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, allowing it to simmer for about 30-40 minutes or until the cabbage is tender.
  6. Final Touch: Check the seasoning and adjust salt and pepper if needed. Remove the bay leaf.
  7. Serve: Ladle the soup into bowls and serve hot with crusty bread or a side salad.
