Sausage Cabbage Soup Recipe

Ingredients:











Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, brown the sausage over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken broth, cabbage, tomatoes (with their juices), basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper to the pot.
  4. Bring the soup to a simmer and let cook until the cabbage is tender, about 20-30 minutes.
  5. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve hot.
