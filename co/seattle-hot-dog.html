Instructions

  1. Prepare the Hot Dogs/Sausages: Split each hot dog or sausage lengthwise while keeping 2 halves together.
  2. Sauté Onions: Heat a sauté or grill pan on high heat. Add oil, onions, and salt and sauté or grill for 2 to 3 minutes or until slightly softened and browned. Ensure you're cooking on high heat for a good golden color and flavor.
  3. Cook the Hot Dogs/Sausages: Remove onions from pan and add the hot dogs or sausages. Sear for about 2 minutes per side until they're heated through and slightly browned or charred. Keep them covered and warm while you toast the buns.
  4. Toast the Buns: Remove the hot dogs or sausages from the pan and add the buns, toasting them split-side down until they're lightly browned.
  5. Assemble the Hot Dogs: Place the hot dogs or sausages in the buns and add the grilled onions to the side. Transfer softened cream cheese to a plastic or piping bag, snip the bottom off, and squeeze the cream cheese over the top. Garnish with fresh jalapeño slices, Sriracha, mustard, and optionally, sauerkraut.
