Ingredients

1 block extra-firm tofu, pressed and drained
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup oat milk
1 cup panko breadcrumbs
2 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp onion powder
1 tsp smoked paprika
1 tsp dried oregano
Salt and pepper to taste
Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Preparation: After pressing and draining the tofu, cut it into bite-sized nugget pieces.
  2. Seasoning Mix: In a bowl, combine the flour, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Dipping Station: Pour oat milk into a separate bowl and place the panko breadcrumbs in another bowl.
  4. Coating: Dip each tofu piece into the flour mixture, ensuring it's fully covered. Then dip it into the oat milk, and finally coat it generously with the panko breadcrumbs.
  5. Frying: Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Once hot, carefully place each tofu nugget into the oil. Fry until golden brown on all sides, which should take about 3-4 minutes per side. Place fried nuggets on a paper towel to drain any excess oil.
  6. Serving: Serve hot with your favourite dipping sauces like ketchup, BBQ sauce, or vegan mayo.

    7. (Note: For an oven-baked version, preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the coated tofu nuggets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until they're golden and crispy.)
