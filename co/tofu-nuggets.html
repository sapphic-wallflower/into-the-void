Tofu Nuggets

Ingredients:














Instructions:

Drain and press tofu

Remove tofu from packaging and drain liquid. Place between two paper towels, and press with heavy object for 30 to 60 minutes. Once finished, cut or tear into nugget shape.

Prepare bedding station

Grab two shallow bowls.

In bowl one, add 1/2 cup of flour, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, 1/2 tsp onion powder, 1/2 tsp paprika, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp black pepper. Mix.

In bowl two, add 1 cup of panko, 2 tbsp of cornstarch, and (optional) 1/4 cup of nutritional yeast. Mix.

Coating your nuggets

Coat each nugget in the flour mix, followed by plant-based milk, followed by panko mix. Set aside.

Fry the nuggets

Heat a liberal amount of cooking oil on a pan over medium heat. Once hot, add nuggets for 2 to 3 minutes, or until golden brown.

Serve and enjoy

Serve with your favourite dippping sauce!
