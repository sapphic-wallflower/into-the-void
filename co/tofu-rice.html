Instructions

  1. Cook the Rice: Rinse the rice until the water runs clear. In a saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add the rice, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes. Once cooked, fluff with a fork and set aside.
  2. Air Fry the Shredded Tofu: Preheat your air fryer to 375°F (190°C). Place the shredded tofu in the air fryer basket and cook for about 15-20 minutes, stirring or shaking occasionally until they're crispy and golden. Transfer to a bowl once done.
  3. Sauté Veggies: In a large skillet over medium heat, sauté the diced onion until translucent. Add the diced bell peppers and sauté for another 4-5 minutes until they start to soften. Finally, add the steamed broccoli and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Assemble the Bowl: Begin by placing a serving of rice at the bottom of the bowl. Top with the crispy shredded tofu and the sautéed veggies.
  5. Curry Drizzle: Warm the premade curry sauce according to its packaging instructions and then drizzle or pour over the assembled bowls.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Optionally garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley for an added touch of freshness.
